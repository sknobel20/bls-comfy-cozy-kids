Why local hospitals

The big-name hospitals already have national donors, gala fundraisers, and corporate sponsors. The smaller, local hospitals down the street — the ones treating kids in our own neighborhoods — often don't. That's where we focus. A handmade card or a small fundraiser goes a long way when nobody else is showing up.
